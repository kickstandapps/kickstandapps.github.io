Get the photo you deserve
with the camera you already have.

  • Full Manual or Automatic

    Shoot in Automatic for great pictures, or get exactly the photo you want by taking direct control of Exposure Value, Shutter Speed, ISO, or Zoom.

  • Prime Priority

    Make only the decisions you want with Exposure, Shutter, and ISO Priority modes. Prime will decide the rest and leave you with a great photo.

  • Seemless Controls

    Switching between Automatic, Manual, and any of the Priority modes is as easy as possible. Just move the slider for the settings you want to control.

  • Focus Zoom

    When you take manual control of your focus, the center of the image is magnified so you can keep your subject sharp.

  • Save to Camera Roll

    Photos save directly to your phone's Camera Roll. From there, you can share, edit, or organize it along with all your other pictures.

  • Geotag Photos

    Save your location with your photos so you know where you were when looking back at your pictures in the future.
